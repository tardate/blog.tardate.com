--- layout: post title: LEAP#206 CD4047 Astable Oscillator date: '2016-06-30T22:26:00.000+08:00' author: Paul Gallagher tags: - LittleArduinoProjects - Electronics modified_time: '2016-06-30T22:26:19.863+08:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-6220586.post-2519105674917414827 blogger_orig_url: http://blog.tardate.com/2016/06/littlearduinoprojects206-cd4047-astable.html --- The CD4047 is capable of running in astable or monostable configurations, with operating frequency configured by an external RC network. So in one sense, sounds like the 555 timer!

Unlike the 555, the CD4047 provides a fixed 50% duty cycle with good frequency stability (+/- 2% @ 100KHz).
